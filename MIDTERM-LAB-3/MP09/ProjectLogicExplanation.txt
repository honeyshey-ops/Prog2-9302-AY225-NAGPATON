MP09 - Display Dataset Statistics
===================================
 
The program asks for the CSV file path and reads all 8 columns per row
(Candidate, Type, Exam, Language, Exam Date, Score, Result, Time Used).
In one loop it collects all statistics at once: PASS/FAIL counts from
Result, score tracking from Score and Candidate, and frequency maps for
Exam, Type, Language, and Exam Date. After the loop, average score and
pass rate are calculated and the full report is printed in sections.
